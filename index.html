Cryptocurrency Overview

Bitcoin

Definition: Bitcoin is a form of digital currency that aims to eliminate the need for central authorities such as banks or governments. Instead, Bitcoin uses blockchain technology to support peer-to-peer transactions between users on a decentralized network.

Binance Coin

Definition: Binance Coin is the crypto-coin issued by the Binance exchange and trades with a BNB symbol. This coin runs on the Ethereum blockchain with standard ERC 20 and is limited to a maximum of 200 million BNB tokens.

Definition: Dogecoin is a cryptocurrency that started as a joke but gained popularity. It features the Shiba Inu dog from the "Doge" meme and is used primarily for tipping and small transactions.
